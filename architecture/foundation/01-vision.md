# Project Overview

Paulera Filmes currently owns a website containing a significant amount of valuable content, including films, videos, photographs and institutional information.

However, the current experience does not reflect the artistic identity of the filmmaker.

Content exists.

Stories exist.

Quality exists.

But there is no narrative capable of guiding visitors through those stories.

The new project is **not a redesign**.

It is a complete rethinking of how Paulera Filmes presents its work to the world.

Instead of creating another portfolio website, this project aims to build a **cinematic digital experience**.

---

# Problem Statement

The current website suffers from several structural problems.

## Information Architecture

Content is presented sequentially without hierarchy.

Projects compete for attention instead of complementing each other.

The visitor has difficulty understanding where to begin.

---

## Storytelling

Although Paulera tells stories professionally, the website itself tells none.

Projects appear as isolated pieces of content.

There is no emotional progression.

No discovery.

No journey.

---

## Visual Identity

The interface functions primarily as a repository.

It communicates information.

It does not communicate personality.

The artistic vision of Paulera is largely absent from the digital experience.

---

## User Experience

Visitors are required to search for interesting work instead of naturally discovering it.

Important projects receive similar visual weight to secondary content.

Navigation favors exploration by chance rather than intentional storytelling.

---

## Emotional Connection

The current experience answers:

> "What has Paulera produced?"

It rarely answers:

> "Who is Paulera?"

This distinction is fundamental.

Clients hire artists because of their vision.

Not because of the number of videos they have produced.

---

# Vision

The new website should feel less like a portfolio and more like entering someone's creative universe.

Visitors should experience a narrative rather than navigate pages.

The interface should encourage contemplation instead of urgency.

Technology should disappear behind storytelling.

Every interaction should reinforce one central idea:

> **Every great story begins long before the camera starts recording.**

---

# Core Concept

The entire experience is built upon a single metaphor:

## The Creative Tree

Every story follows a natural lifecycle.

It begins beneath the surface.

It grows through experience.

It expands into different directions.

It bears fruit.

Those fruits leave seeds for future stories.

This metaphor becomes the foundation of:

- Brand identity
- Navigation
- Information architecture
- Motion design
- Visual language
- Content hierarchy
- User journey

The tree is **not** a decorative 3D object.

The tree **is the experience itself**.

---

# Project Goals

## Primary Goal

Create a memorable digital experience capable of emotionally connecting visitors with Paulera's artistic identity before presenting individual projects.

---

## Secondary Goals

- Present Paulera as an author, not simply as a service provider.

- Organize existing content through meaningful storytelling.

- Increase perceived value through presentation rather than quantity.

- Encourage visitors to explore multiple projects.

- Create an experience people remember after leaving the website.

- Build a portfolio that naturally evolves as new work is produced.

---

# Non Goals

This project does **not** aim to become:

- a streaming platform

- a YouTube replacement

- a Vimeo replacement

- a social network

- an infinite content feed

- a dashboard

- a SaaS-style marketing website

- a technological showcase

Technology exists only to support narrative.

# User Expectations

Visitors typically arrive expecting to answer one or more of the following questions:

- Who is Paulera?

- What kind of films does he create?

- Is his work aligned with my project?

- Can I trust this professional?

- How do I contact him?

The experience should answer these questions naturally without overwhelming the visitor.

# Experience Principles

These principles guide every future decision.

---

## Story Over Technology

Technology must never become the protagonist.

Animation only exists when it strengthens storytelling.

If an effect distracts from the story, it should be removed.

---

## Every Scroll Has Meaning

Scrolling is not transportation.

Scrolling is progression.

Every movement represents a new chapter.

Nothing exists merely to occupy vertical space.

---

## Less But Better

Showing fewer projects with deeper storytelling creates greater perceived value than displaying everything equally.

Quality beats quantity.

Always.

---

## Curiosity Before Information

The interface should encourage discovery.

Visitors should want to continue exploring.

Information should unfold naturally instead of appearing all at once.

---

## Calm Interfaces

Nothing should feel rushed.

The experience breathes.

Animations respect silence.

Whitespace is intentional.

Transitions are smooth.

Visitors should feel invited to stay.

---

## Organic Systems

Nothing should feel mechanical.

Spacing.

Motion.

Shapes.

Transitions.

Lighting.

Everything should suggest life rather than machinery.

---

## Timeless Design

Avoid visual trends that may quickly become outdated.

The interface should feel contemporary without depending on fashionable aesthetics.

---

# Success Metrics

This project succeeds if visitors:

- remember the experience after leaving

- understand Paulera's artistic identity

- explore multiple projects

- spend significantly longer browsing compared to the previous website

- feel encouraged to establish contact

The project does **not** measure success solely through page views.

Emotional impact matters.

---

# Constraints

## Content

Existing projects should be preserved whenever possible.

New content structure should emerge from organization rather than unnecessary recreation.

---

## Performance

Animations must remain performant.

The experience should feel cinematic without compromising responsiveness.

Performance is a design feature.

---

## Accessibility

Visual sophistication must never compromise accessibility.

Motion should respect reduced-motion preferences.

Contrast should remain readable.

Keyboard navigation should be supported.

---

## Scalability

The website should support future projects without redesigning its architecture.

Each new project should naturally become another fruit on the tree.

---

# Design Philosophy

This project is not inspired by corporate websites.

It is inspired by cinema.

Visitors should never feel like they are navigating software.

They should feel like they are walking through a living forest filled with stories.

---

# Guiding Statement

> **Technology should never attract more attention than the story it exists to tell.**

Every future decision should be validated against this statement.

# Decision Log

## D-001

### Decision

The website will be designed around the metaphor of a living tree.

### Reason

This metaphor naturally represents creativity, artistic growth, storytelling and continuous evolution.

### Impact

Navigation, architecture, motion, branding and content organization will all derive from this single concept.

---

## D-002

### Decision

The Home page will function as a narrative experience instead of a traditional landing page.

### Reason

Visitors should emotionally connect with Paulera before evaluating individual projects.

### Impact

The homepage becomes a guided journey rather than a collection of independent sections.

---

## D-003

### Decision

Individual projects will exist as dedicated Case pages.

### Reason

Each project deserves sufficient depth to communicate its creative process and value.

### Impact

The Home page introduces stories.

Case pages tell them completely.
