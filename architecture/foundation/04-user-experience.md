# User Experience

> This document defines the complete user journey.
>
> It should be interpreted as the screenplay of the experience.
>
> It explains what visitors see, feel and understand at every stage of the journey.
>
> UI comes later.
>
> Components come later.
>
> Development comes later.
>
> Here we define the story.

---

# Purpose

Most websites organize information.

This project organizes emotions.

The objective is not to guide users through pages.

The objective is to guide them through a creative journey.

Every interaction exists to reinforce one idea:

> Creativity grows like a tree.

The visitor should never consciously think about this metaphor.

They should simply feel it.

---

# Narrative Structure

The journey is divided into five chapters.

```text
Arrival

↓

Roots

↓

Trunk

↓

Branches

↓

Fruits

↓

Seeds
```

Each chapter has a different emotional objective.

---

# Emotional Progression

| Chapter | Visitor Emotion |
|----------|----------------|
| Arrival | Curiosity |
| Roots | Connection |
| Trunk | Trust |
| Branches | Discovery |
| Fruits | Admiration |
| Seeds | Inspiration |

The website should slowly move visitors from curiosity to action.

Never rush this process.

---

# CHAPTER 01 — Arrival

## Narrative Purpose

Introduce mystery.

Do not explain everything.

Invite exploration.

---

## What the visitor sees

A forest.

Almost silent.

Soft ambient movement.

The Creative Tree appears.

Not completely illuminated.

Only partially revealed.

The visitor immediately understands:

"This is not a conventional portfolio."

---

## Hero Copy

Example

"Toda grande história nasce antes da câmera."

Alternative

"Nem toda história começa quando alguém aperta REC."

---

## Motion

Very subtle camera breathing.

Leaves moving.

Soft wind.

Light passing through branches.

No dramatic animations.

---

## User Goal

Encourage scrolling.

Nothing else.

The Hero should never overwhelm visitors with information.

---

# Scroll Transition

Instead of jumping to another section,

the camera slowly approaches the roots.

The tree remains present.

The world changes.

Not the page.

---

## Narrative Meaning

Everything invisible.

Identity.

Purpose.

Origin.

---

## Visitor Question

Who is Paulera?

---

## Content

Manifesto.

Creative philosophy.

Values.

Growth.

Experience.

Trust.

---

## Content

Timeline.

Important milestones.

Clients.

Recognition.

Awards.

Artistic versatility.

# CHAPTER 05 — Fruits

## Narrative Meaning

Completed stories.

Creative achievements.

Projects.

Legacy.

---

## Visitor Question

Can I see the work?

---

## Structure

Featured Cases.

Not every project.

Quality over quantity.

Each Case should receive enough space to breathe.

---

## Card Behavior

Never look like a portfolio grid.

Cards should feel like exhibition pieces.

Minimal text.

Clear hierarchy.

Slow hover animations.

## Interaction

Hover

Small camera movement.

Soft lighting.

Subtle parallax.

Click

The camera follows.

# Case Transition

This transition deserves special attention.

The visitor should never feel

"I opened another page."

Instead

"I entered another story."

The camera follows the fruit.

The fruit fills the screen.

It slowly transforms into the Case Hero.

No fade.

No instant loading.

Narrative continuity.

---

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

---

# Return Transition

Returning from a Case should place visitors back at the same fruit.

Context should never be lost.

---

# Ending

After the CTA,

the camera slowly returns to the entire tree.

---

# Scroll Philosophy

Scrolling represents time.

Not navigation.

The page should never feel infinite.

Every movement should communicate progress.

---

# Continuous Journey

Users should never perceive the homepage as independent sections.

Instead, each scroll movement advances the virtual camera.

The visitor remains inside the same environment from beginning to end.

Every transition should preserve spatial continuity.

The objective is immersion rather than navigation.

---

# Motion Rules

Movement follows nature.

Nothing accelerates abruptly.

Nothing bounces.

Nothing rotates unnecessarily.

Nothing exists only because it looks impressive.

Motion tells stories.

---

# Interaction Rules

Hover reveals.

Click commits.

Scroll progresses.

Nothing should surprise users negatively.

Interactions should feel predictable,

yet magical.

---

# Accessibility

The narrative must work without animations.

Reduced Motion should preserve storytelling through layout and typography.

Nothing essential should depend on motion.

---

# Mobile Experience

Mobile is not a simplified desktop.

It is another camera.

The journey remains identical.

Only framing changes.

---

# UX Principles

Visitors should feel guided.

Not controlled.

Curious.

Not confused.

Inspired.

Not overwhelmed.

---

# Decision Log

## D-010

### Decision

The website will follow a cinematic narrative instead of conventional navigation.

### Reason

Narrative increases memorability.

### Impact

Every interaction becomes part of the story.

---

## D-011

### Decision

The tree remains present throughout the experience.

### Reason

Maintaining the same visual anchor reinforces immersion.

### Impact

Future layouts should never abandon the central metaphor.

---

## D-012

### Decision

Cases become independent narrative experiences.

### Reason

Each project deserves its own emotional rhythm.

### Impact

Case pages should never resemble standard portfolio pages.

# AI Context

This document defines the screenplay of the product.

Do not optimize for speed alone.

Optimize for rhythm.

Every future layout should answer:

"What is the visitor feeling at this moment?"

If a future design interrupts emotional progression, reconsider it.

Narrative continuity has priority over interface novelty.
