# ADR-0006

Status

Accepted

---

# Title

Three.js is used only where it strengthens the narrative.

---

# Decision

Three.js is reserved for:

Hero Tree.

Ambient interactions.

Subtle transitions.

Never for decorative purposes.

---

# Avoid

Entire pages in WebGL.

Heavy shaders.

Blocking loading.
