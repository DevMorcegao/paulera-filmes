# ADR-0004

Status

Accepted

---

# Title

Homepage follows a continuous storytelling structure.

---

# Context

Visitors should experience the filmmaker's journey before exploring individual works.

---

# Decision

The homepage is a narrative.

Not a traditional landing page.

Cases remain independent pages.

---

# Alternatives

Corporate homepage.

Portfolio grid.

Multi-page navigation.

---

# Consequences

Higher emotional impact.

Better narrative.

More scroll choreography.
